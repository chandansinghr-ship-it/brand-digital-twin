1. Activated Charcoal Smoothie			
Ingredients (1 serving):			
Activated charcoal powder – ½ tsp			
Banana – 1 medium (ripe)			
Almond milk / low-fat milk – 200 ml			
Honey – 1 tsp (optional)			
Chia seeds – 1 tsp			
Ice cubes – 4–5			
			
Method:			
1. Add banana, milk, honey, and charcoal powder to blender.			
2. Blend until smooth and creamy.			
3. Add chia seeds, blend once quickly.			
4. Serve chilled in a glass with ice cubes.			
			
			
2. Aglio Olio (o) – Veg			
Ingredients (1 portion):			
Spaghetti pasta – 120 g (boiled al dente)			
Olive oil – 2 tbsp			
Garlic – 6–8 cloves (sliced)			
Dry red chili flakes – ½ tsp			
Zucchini, bell peppers, broccoli – ½ cup (mixed, cut into strips)			
Parsley – 1 tbsp (chopped)			
Salt – to taste			
Black pepper – ¼ tsp			
Parmesan cheese (optional) – 1 tbsp			
			
Method:			
1. Heat olive oil, sauté sliced garlic until golden.			
2. Add chili flakes and sauté vegetables until slightly tender.			
3. Toss in boiled spaghetti, salt, pepper.			
4. Mix parsley and a splash of pasta water for emulsification.			
5. Serve hot with optional parmesan.			
			
			
			
3. Aglio Olio (o) – Chicken			
Ingredients (1 portion):			
Spaghetti pasta – 120 g (boiled al dente)			
Olive oil – 2 tbsp			
Garlic – 6–8 cloves (sliced)			
Chili flakes – ½ tsp			
Grilled chicken breast – 100 g (sliced strips)			
Parsley – 1 tbsp (chopped)			
Salt – to taste			
Black pepper – ¼ tsp			
Parmesan cheese (optional) – 1 tbsp			
			
Method:			
1. Heat olive oil, sauté garlic until golden.			
2. Add chili flakes, then chicken strips, stir-fry 2–3 minutes.			
3. Add boiled spaghetti, salt, pepper, toss well.			
4. Add parsley and a splash of pasta water to coat.			
5. Serve hot with parmesan if required.			
			
4. Aglio Olio (o) – Prawns			
Ingredients (1 portion):

5. Alfredo Pasta (o) – Veg			
Ingredients (1 portion):			
Spaghetti/Fettuccine pasta – 120 g (boiled al dente)			
Olive oil – 1 tbsp			
Garlic – 5 g (minced)			
Butter – 10 g			
Fresh cream – 100 ml			
Parmesan cheese – 20 g			
Mixed vegetables (broccoli, zucchini, bell peppers) – 70 g			
Salt – to taste			
Black pepper – ¼ tsp			
Parsley – 1 tbsp (chopped)			
			
Method:			
1. Heat oil + butter, sauté garlic until fragrant.			
2. Add mixed vegetables, stir-fry lightly.			
3. Add cream, parmesan, salt, pepper, stir well.			
4. Toss in boiled pasta with a splash of pasta water.			
5. Garnish with parsley & serve hot.
