This Privacy Policy explains how `Trending Media Service Pvt. Ltd.` ("**we**", "**us**") collects,
uses, shares, and protects personal data when you use the Brand Digital Twin platform
(the "**Service**"). It is incorporated into our Terms of Service.

**Roles.** For data about your own account users we act as a **controller**. For the
advertising, commerce, and accounting data you connect to operate the Service, we act
as a **processor** on your behalf — that processing is governed by our **Data
Processing Addendum (DPA)**.

## 1. Data we collect

**a) Account and identity data** (controller): organization name, user name, email,
hashed password, role, authentication and session data, and your acceptance records
for legal documents.

**b) Connected platform data** (processor): metrics and records from sources you
connect, which may include — advertising metrics (spend, clicks, impressions,
conversions, campaign and ad-set structure); commerce data (orders, revenue, refunds,
discounts, fulfillment and marketplace fees, product and variant data); cost data
(unit costs / cost of goods); and, depending on the platform, **transaction-level
identifiers such as click IDs or order totals** used to compute profitability and
attribution.

**c) Connection credentials**: OAuth access and refresh tokens for platforms you
connect, held in an encrypted credential vault.

**d) Billing data**: subscription status, the amount you suggest, and payment
references. Card payments are tokenized by our payment processor; **we do not store
full card numbers.**

**e) Usage, device, and log data**: actions you take in the Service, recommendation
events (shown / approved / executed / dismissed / reversed) used to measure outcomes,
IP address, browser/device information, and diagnostic logs. We anonymize/redact
identifiers and tokens in error events.

**f) Cookies**: see Section 7.

## 2. How and why we use data (legal bases)

We use data to: provide and secure the Service; compute POAS and diagnostics; generate
and (where you authorize) execute recommendations within your Trust Tier and spend
caps; process billing; provide support; measure and improve the Service; and comply
with law and enforce our Terms.

As an India-based service, our primary framework is the **Digital Personal Data
Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP)**: we process personal data on the basis of your consent
(obtained via a clear Consent Notice) or other legally permitted grounds, and we honor
withdrawal of consent. Where GDPR/UK GDPR applies (for example, EU/UK data subjects in
connected data), our legal bases are **performance of a contract** (to provide the
Service), **legitimate interests** (to secure, measure, and improve the Service,
balanced against your rights), **consent** (for non-essential cookies and certain
communications), and **legal obligation**. *(Counsel: confirm DPDP Consent Notice
wording and GDPR bases.)*

We do **not** sell personal data, and we do not use Customer Data to train models for
other customers without your instruction. *(Counsel/Company: confirm this commitment.)*

## 3. Advertising-platform compliance (Consent Mode)

Where we transmit or process signals for advertising platforms, we support
**Google Consent Mode v2** and redact or withhold identifiers in line with the consent
state and applicable platform requirements.

advertising, commerce, and accounting platforms you connect. A current list is at

Terms, or in a merger or acquisition (with notice where required).

We retain account data for `[RETENTION — ACTIVE]` and, after account deletion,

## 7. Cookies and similar technologies

We use **essential** cookies for authentication and security, which are on by default.
Non-essential (analytics/performance) cookies are used only with your consent via our
cookie banner; the default is essential-only. You can change your choice at any time.
See the banner and your browser controls for management options.

## 8. Your rights

Subject to applicable law you may have the right to access, correct, delete, export
(portability), restrict or object to processing, and withdraw consent. The Service
provides in-product **data export** (a signed export of your data) and **account
deletion** (a hard-delete cascade that also revokes connected credentials). To exercise
other rights or appeal a decision, contact `admin@trendingmediagroup.in`. India residents may
contact our Grievance Officer / DPO below; EU/UK residents may also lodge a complaint
with a supervisory authority.

## 9. Security

We implement measures including encryption in transit and at rest, **request-scoped
database access to enforce tenant isolation**, an encrypted credential vault, secret
management, redaction/anonymization of identifiers and tokens in error events, access
controls, and audit logging. No method is perfectly secure, but we work to protect
your data and will notify affected parties of qualifying breaches as required by law.

## 10. Children

The Service is for business use and not directed to children. We do not knowingly
collect personal data from children.

We may update this Policy. For material changes we will provide notice and, where
